Puthenchira Government Hospital in Puthenchira, Thrissur has a well-equipped clinic with all the modern equipment. The clinic has separate waiting and consultation areas which allow enough space for patients to wait conveniently at the clinic. Being a specialized Hospitals, the doctor offers a number of medical services. A substantial share of the credit for Puthenchira's health achievements goes to the remarkable reach of the health care system within the Village. The special distribution of the health care institution enables the people to make use of the system of their choice which generally available within easy reach. Puthenchira Community Health Centre (CHC Puthenchira), Thrissur District, Kerala situated just one km away from Puthenchira High School Junction which is easily accessible from any part of the village. It is well known health care and prevention centre to the common people from the nearby areas and villages of Puthenchira Panchayat. This health institution started as a Primary Health Centre and upgraded as Community Health Centre a few years ago. Currently Puthenchira Community Health Centre (Puthenchira CHC) has 3 consultant doctors, 2 Health Supervisors and supporting staffs.
